A bit about me...

I'm a developer in training through _nology, building on my tech foundation and my love for clean, aesthetic design. I completed my Bachelor's in Electronics and Instrumentation, which eventually led me into the tech industry as a QA Analyst. Along the way, I also ran and curated content for my own design-inspiration websites, a space where I fine-tuned my eye for detail, user experience, and what makes digital content genuinely engaging.

I'm a big foodie at heart. I love discovering new places to eat and experimenting in the kitchen, and trying new recipes has always come naturally to me. I also enjoy travelling (even though I haven't explored as much as I'd like to yet), unwinding with films and TV shows, and dreaming up interior design ideas. Anything aesthetic instantly catches my attention.

This portfolio is a reflection of everything I'm learning and building, and it will keep evolving as I grow as a developer and as a creator.
